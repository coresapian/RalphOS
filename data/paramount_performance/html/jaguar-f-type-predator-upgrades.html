Jaguar F-Type Predator

  • Power: 675 HP

Presenting the Jaguar F-Type Predator 675.

The Jaguar F-Type Predator runs at an impressive 675 horsepower from a 550 horsepower base engine.

There are two main components of the Jaguar F-Type Predator conversion package: styling components and performance components. The first half makes it look fast while the other half makes it go fast. Get a piece of the Predator for yourself with VIEZU, at our International HQ in Warwickshire, or via international mail order shipped straight to you, or a garage of your choosing!

Jaguar F-Type Tuning & Styling

With a remarkable 675 horsepower and redesigned styling, the Jaguar F-Type Predator commands attention everywhere it goes. The Jaguar F-Type Predator Package is now available for your own F-Type with professional installation at our state-of-the-art headquarters in Bidford on Avon, Warwickshire, or via worldwide mail order.

Jaguar Performance Upgrades

Depending on the specific model and derivative the Jaguar F-Type Predator tuning package can deliver up to 675 BHP.

You can rely on Paramount Performance and VIEZUs long-standing reputation when it comes to mechanical and software upgrades. The Jaguar tuning process is also referred to as chip tuning, remapping and tuning.

Our own Jaguar F-Type Predator development vehicle made astounding power. Below is the dyno chart for the Jaguar Predator 675 upgrade package for the Jaguar F-Type. Paramount Performance and VIEZU are Jaguar F-Type Predator tuning specialists and have huge experience with these cars. Our team also owns and drives them!

As you can see, below graph Min, Max, and Average (Avg) figures. The Max and Avg figures show the gains between test 1 (the original software run) and test 2 (the upgraded vehicle run) an impressive gain all round!

Rolling Road (Dyno) Tested

Paramount Performance and VIEZUs software and hardware modifications are thoroughly tested using VIEZUs state of the art 3,000 BHP four wheel drive rolling road. As a result, modifications and engine tuning can be fine-tuned as needed. Dyno testing and fine-tuning provide the best performance and power for your Jaguar F-Type.

Jaguar Styling

Right from the start, show off your style. You can customise your Jaguar F-Type with a number of carbon fibre parts.

  • Carbon Fibre Front Splitter Make a bold statement with a sleek and stylish carbon fibre front splitter.
  • You can also install the carbon fibre rear diffuser to match your side skirts and continue your carbon fibre theme.
  • This rear diffuser matches the front splitter and side skirts in carbon fibre and is the final piece in the set.
  • Carbon Fibre Dashboard The F-Type is an elegant vehicle, but there is always room for improvement. Adding a carbon fibre dashboard will lift up the interior.
  • Also available, Carbon Fibre Boot Spoilers for the Coupe and Convertible
  • Carbon Fibre Door Mirror Covers

Further personalisation options

  • Exclusive Sports Exhaust System
  • Bespoke alloy wheel options

Professional fitting at our Workshop

All upgrades, including the legendary Jaguar F-Type Predator package can be installed by our experienced technicians at our state-of-the-art workshop in Bidford on Avon, Warwickshire, UK.

Worldwide Mail order
