The Top Trucks/SUVs of Super Summit 2013

Posted 06/28/13 by OnAllCylinders Staff
Last updated on 08/16/2024

We love contests almost as much as we love car shows.

That’s why we want you to vote for yourfavorite cars and trucks of SuperSummit 2013. That’s right–the cruise-in at Super Summitwas a non-judged affair, so we’re making you an honorary judge in the OnAllCylinders Super Summit Best of Show contest. Simply watch the OnAllCylinders, Summit Racing , and Summit Racing Trucks Facebookpages for the Top 10cars and trucksand “Like” and “Share” your favorite vehicle. Comments are welcome, too. In fact, you can also vote by commenting below.

Here are the finalists in the car category as selected by the OnAllCylinders staff (see thecar finalists here):

1965 GMC Suburban

1948 Ford F-1

Chevrolet 3200

1958 Chevrolet Suburban Apache

1938 Chevrolet 1/2-Ton

1955 Ford F-100

Willys Pickup

1934 Dodge Pickup

1967 Chevrolet C-10

Dodge Ram 3500 Dually
