2007 Nissan 350Z

2007 Nissan 350Z Base
Chad Hannan
Updated July 30, 2025

The Story

This is my 2007 HR 350z base model. Car is sitting around 36k miles. Some modifications I have done to this build include Airlift 3p, custom 2p forged wheels with carbon aero discs, carbon hood, carbon louvers, and miscellaneous aero.

Modifications

Suspension

  • AirLift Performance 3P Air Suspension Kit

Performance

  • Berk HFC/ISR duals

Exterior

  • Subzero carbon hood, carbon louvers
  • Devsport splitters
  • KRNC 2p custom design with carbon fiber aero discs
