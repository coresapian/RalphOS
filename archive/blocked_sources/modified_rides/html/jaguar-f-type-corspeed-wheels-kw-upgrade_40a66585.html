Jaguar F-Type Gets a Stunning Makeover with Cor.Speed Wheels

By shawn On 24/03/2025 at 09:48 0

In Modified Cars

The Jaguar F-Type has always been a head-turner, but this custom build takes it to a whole new level. Swiss tuning experts at Tridende GmbH have transformed this V8-powered coupe with a striking set of Cor.Speed Deville wheels and a KW suspension upgrade. The result? A sleek, aggressive stance that enhances both aesthetics and performance.

When Jaguar launched the F-Type in 2012, it wasn't just introducing a new sports car it was reviving a legacy. Inspired by the legendary E-Type of the '60s and '70s, the F-Type carried forward the spirit of its predecessor with a sleek, long-hooded design available in both coupe and roadster form. Over the years, it evolved, and by the time of its major facelift in 2019, it had cemented itself as a true modern classic.

The example featured here is a later model from the post-2019 update, given a refined, performance-driven upgrade that enhances both its aesthetics and handling. Swiss tuning experts at Tridende GmbH, based in Schlatt, Thurgau, took this V8-powered coupe and elevated its presence with a striking set of Cor.Speed Deville wheels.

Cor.Speed Deville Wheels: A Bold Upgrade

The transformation starts with a set of premium Cor.Speed Deville wheels, known for their bold yet elegant design. Featuring ten straight, muscular spokes finished in a brushed silver coat, these wheels add a distinct edge to the F-Type’s aggressive stance. Up front, they measure 9x21 inches with a concave profile, while the rear boasts a wider 10.5x21-inch setup with extreme concavity. The wheel package is wrapped in high-performance tires 255/30ZR21 at the front and 295/25ZR21 at the rear offering a perfect balance of grip and style.

Optimized Stance with KW Adjustable Springs

A stunning wheel upgrade is only complete when paired with the right suspension setup, and Tridende GmbH made sure the Jaguar F-Type sits just right. To achieve the perfect stance and optimize handling, they installed a KW height-adjustable spring kit. This subtle but essential modification ensures the wheels fit seamlessly within the arches while improving the car’s dynamics on the road.

The Jaguar F-Type has always been a head-turner, but with this refined setup, it now boasts an even more commanding presence. The combination of Cor.Speed Deville wheels and a lowered stance not only enhances its visual appeal but also improves performance, giving the driver a more engaging and responsive ride.

For those looking to take their F-Type to the next level, this build proves that the right modifications can blend elegance with performance effortlessly. With a design that echoes Jaguar’s rich heritage and modern enhancements that refine its character, this F-Type is a perfect example of how classic inspiration meets contemporary tuning.
